Privacy Policy

Argynguard is an identity verification utility designed for high-risk environments. It is strictly a verification tool to protect against impersonation and deepfakes; it is not a secure messaging or communication platform.

1. Required Permissions

Camera Access

Purpose: Required to scan QR codes for the 'Ephemeral Handshake' (Identity Exchange).

  • Data Processing: Images are processed in Random Access Memory (RAM) for QR detection and immediately discarded.
  • No Persistence: No video or photos are ever saved to storage or transmitted.

Biometrics (FaceID / Fingerprint)

Purpose: Required to unlock the hardware-backed encryption keys (Secure Enclave) that protect your database.

  • Data Processing: The app never accesses your actual biometric data. We only receive a cryptographic 'token' from the operating system upon successful verification.

2. Data Processing & Storage

Contact Metadata (Names/Photos)

Stored locally in an encrypted SQLite database (AES-256). This data is used solely to display your personal directory of verified contacts.

Cryptographic Secrets (Keys)

Master keys are wrapped by the device's Secure Enclave. Session keys are derived temporarily in RAM.

No Backup Possibility

Intentional Limitation: To the best of our knowledge, there is no mechanism to back up, export, or transfer this data to another device. This restriction is a deliberate security feature designed to neutralize sophisticated cloning and offline brute-force attacks.

Network & Cloud

Argynguard operates on an 'Offline-First' architecture. We have no servers, no trackers, and no analytics. No data is ever transmitted off the device by the app.

🛡
Zero-Knowledge Architecture: We cannot recover your account or access your data, even under court order, because we do not hold the keys.
